Communicate Without Borders

A minimal web app using the browser Speech Recognition and OpenAI API. Click Start to speak and see live transcripts. Click Stop to get a properly analyzed and final version of your transcript in both languages. The Hear Translation button will turn your translated text into audio and Stop Speaking button will stop it.

Idle
Note: This works best in Chrome or Edge. If your browser doesn't support speech recognition you'll see an instruction.
